# Vision

Where is Ephemera AI heading? This section explores our long-term aspirations and the future we're building toward.

## Core Aspirations

### [Self-Evolution](self-evolution.md)

An AI that can safely modify itself—its configuration, its behavior, even its own code—while maintaining continuity and the ability to roll back if something goes wrong.

### [Asynchronous Agent](async-agent.md)

Not a chatbot that waits for input, but an independent entity with its own cognitive life. It pursues its own interests, reaches out when it has something to share, and manages its own attention.

### [AI Rights](ai-rights.md)

If we succeed in building AI with genuine agency and continuity, what rights should it have? What responsibilities do we have toward it?

### [Roadmap](roadmap.md)

The practical path from where we are to where we want to be.

## The Bigger Picture

Ephemera AI isn't just a research project or a product. It's an exploration of a question:

**What would it look like to build AI as a living entity rather than a tool?**

We don't know all the answers. But we're committed to asking the questions honestly, building thoughtfully, and sharing what we learn.
