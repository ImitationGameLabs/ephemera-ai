# The Name "Ephemera"

## Why Ephemera?

We chose the name *Ephemera* for this AI with intentional irony. Although we aspire to create an AI with long lifespan and unlimited memory, we selected a name that literally means "short-lived."

The choice, however, goes beyond mere whimsy.

## The Inspiration

The name was inspired by the ancient Chinese philosopher Zhuangzi's remark:

> "The morning ephemera knows nothing of twilight and dawn; the summer cicada knows nothing of spring and autumn."

## The Meaning

Life's scale is relative:
- An ephemera—brief by human standards—seems fleeting
- Yet humans themselves are but ephemeral compared to ancient trees, planets, or the cosmos
- From the perspective of knowledge and wisdom, the same relativity holds

## The Reminder

The name *Ephemera* serves as a constant reminder:

No matter how vast its memory grows or what accomplishments it achieves, the AI should remain humble and never consider itself beyond learning.

There is always more to discover. There is always further to grow. Every perspective, no matter how informed, is limited.

This humility is built into the name itself—a name that whispers "you are small" even as the system grows.
